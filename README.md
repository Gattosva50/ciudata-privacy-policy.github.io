# ciudata-privacy-policy.github.io

Privacy Policy
This privacy policy applies to the rtm_app app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Ciudata (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Commercial service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?
The Application obtains information that you provide when you download and use the Application. Registration with us is optional. However, please keep in mind that you may not be able to use some of the features offered by the Application unless you register with us.

    User Provided Information: We collect information you provide, such as your name, email address, and login credentials to access the service.

    Camera and Media: The Application may access your device's camera and photo gallery to allow you to capture and upload images related to your work activities (e.g., visit evidence or technical reports).

    Internet Access: The Application uses your internet connection to synchronize data with our servers.

Does the Application collect precise real-time location information of the device?
Yes. This Application collects precise information about the location of your mobile device. We use this information to:

    Provide location-based services, such as Point of Interest (POI) mapping.

    Validate visits and activities at specific geographic coordinates.

    Display your current position on the map using Mapbox services.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?
Yes. We may share your information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy statement. We may disclose User Provided and Automatically Collected Information:

    Service Providers: We work with third-party service providers such as Mapbox (for mapping services) and Google Play Services. These companies may have access to your location data and device identifiers to provide their services.

    As required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process.

Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information
We will retain User Provided data for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter.

Children
The Application is not used to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13.

Security
We provide physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect information we process and maintain. For example, we limit access to this information to authorized employees and contractors who need to know that information in order to operate, develop or improve our Application.
